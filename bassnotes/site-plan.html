Bass Clef Notes Flashcards Site Plan

Westley Bateman

WDD 131.01

Overview

Purpose

Allow people to quiz themsleves on the names of music notes in base clef.

Audience

People that want to see how well they know the names of the notes in base clef.

Dynamic elements

I am going to do a flashcard type thing. If a user clicks in a certain spot on the page, a card will flip over and show something different. I am also going to put buttons that let a user see a different card.
